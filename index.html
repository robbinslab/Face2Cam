WEyeDS Dataset

We introduce the Webcam Eye Dataset (WEyeDS), a dataset of im-
ages collected from desktops and laptops. WEyeDS will be openly
available with the goal of providing researchers a dataset for train-
ing gaze estimation models. Our preliminary dataset was collected
from 38 participants who completed prosaccade tasks on their per-
sonal desktop or laptop computer.
